To all attendees

Oh man, it's almost hacking time! I'm sure we're all quite excited to get going, but there are some things you should be aware of beforehand.

Things to bring

  • Student ID
  • Completed waiver
  • Computer, phone, chargers, and headphones
  • Sleeping bag
  • Optional change of clothes, toiletries
  • Any gadgets you want to use in projects
  • Positive energy!

We provide food and drink.

Wifi

The venue wifi will not be able to support all attendees without slowing down significantly. Thus, we ask that you download all necessary software and documentation ahead of time and use hotspots / turn off wifi when possible. Even better, bring a router! These measures will ensure that everyone has the optimal hacking experience.

Final notes

Important links are in the sidebar activated by . Make sure to join the Slack and sign the waiver before coming to Informatica. It is also highly recommended that you get the iOS app to receive push notifications during the event.
